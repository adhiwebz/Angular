Competetion


Moonwalk

Moon walk is an All Kerala free style group dance competition conducted by Sarga Kshetra. The participants come under the age group 15 to 25.It is well appreciated by the general public and the institutions. It is a free style group dance, so there will be Varity of dances; classical, semi classical, western, cinematic, and thematic and so on. That is the one of the major attraction of both the participant teams and the spectators. Moon Walk is a free style group dance competition. The dance form can be of classical, semi classical, western, cinematic or thematic.

Gloria

Gloria is an All Kerala Ecumenical Carol singing competition conducted by Sarga Kshetra. It is well appreciated by the general public and the institutions. It is an ecumenical competition so there will be Varity of singing styles there is no age limit so there will be variety of groups. We give waitage in judgment for the presentation style, stage props and even for the theme. So that we got an amazing verities of presentations. The competition is very spectacular and appears in T.V dur4ing Christmas season. Along with Gloria, we conduct Sarga-Santa the santaclouse competition for kids. This is also well appreciated by the spectators

Niracharth

NiraCharth is an All Kerala painting competition conducted by Sarga Kshetra Along with Gloria, we conduct Sarga-Santa the santaclouse competition for kids. This is also well appreciated by the spectators

Litereary Competetion

1. C.C.A
Most of the reputed schools in Kottayam District have been provided with team of teachers and specialists by ‘Sarga Kshetra’ to conduct C.C.A programme effectively. We have teachers to teach musical instruments like Piano, keyboard, Violin, Guitar, Tabala and Drums. We also have dance and music teachers. We also provide teachers to train budding artists with painting drawing mimicry and martial arts

3. Competitions
Sarga Kshetra labours to discover wings for the seniors, the middle aged and give training to the young and enable them look at life cheerfully. We conduct many competitions in music, dance, painting and literature.

ABOUT

Sarga kshetra is the breeding ground of the creative talents of the young generation. It is a fertile ground (kshetra) to cultivate and develop native human potentials and creativity (sarga).Inspired by the vision and heritage of Blessed Kuriakose Elias Chavara, one of the founding fathers of the cmi congregation, the cmi father have been actively engaged in providing holistic and integral development to individuals through education and socio-cultural activities.
